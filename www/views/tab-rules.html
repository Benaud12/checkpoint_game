Rules

A fantastic electronic version of "Hotter, Colder" or "Hunt The Thimble", you must find a number of locations based only on a highly stylised map and some cryptic clues... are you up to the challenge??

Click Start New Game to get started and follow each clue to the location, giuded by the colour of the relevant checkpoint on the map.

Click Check In to test how close you are to each checkpoint and to update the colour guiding you!

Good luck and have fun!
